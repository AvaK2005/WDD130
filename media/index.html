MOVIES

The Truman Show is a movie that stays with you long after you watch it. It’s completely mind-boggling and incredible interesting. It’s hard to put it to words so you’ve just got to watch it and find out.

Speed Racer is a beautiful Action/Coming of Age film that is built on themes of Hope, relationships, and determination. It’s beautifully colorful and full of whimsical vibes that are the best for a fun family get together.

Everything Everywhere All at Once is an intense and heartfelt action that pulls you right into the story. The family feels in this film is enough to make a grown man cry, and if not, there is plenty of hilarious and absurd situations to soften even the coldest of hearts.
